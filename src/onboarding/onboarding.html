Welcome to Mixcloud (with) Tracklist v3

Welcome to the new version of our Mixcloud Tracklist Extension! Due to changes on Mixcloud's website, we've updated our extension. Now, you can access and interact with tracklists through the extension's browser popup, rather than directly on the Mixcloud site. Simply click the extension icon to view the tracklist of any Mixcloud mix. You can still conveniently play tracks directly from this popup. Enjoy an uninterrupted Mixcloud experience with our updated functionality!
